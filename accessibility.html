Accessibility

Last updated: June 28, 2026

H2O2U Bulk Water Delivery is committed to making our website usable for everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as a practical standard for accessible design.

What We've Done

This site is built with accessibility in mind, including:

  • Semantic HTML structure for clear navigation by assistive technology
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images
  • Color choices intended to maintain readable contrast
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, menus, and interactive elements
  • A responsive layout that adapts to phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Reduced-motion support that respects your device preferences

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time task. We continue to review and improve the site as we find areas that can be better, and as standards and tools evolve.

Third-Party Content

Some features rely on third-party tools, such as our form provider. We cannot fully control the accessibility of outside services, but we choose providers that support accessible use where we can.

Tell Us About a Problem

If you run into any part of this site that is difficult to use, we want to know. Call us at (512) 695-5204 or reach out through our contact page, and we'll do our best to help and to fix the issue.
