Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

H2O2U Bulk Water Delivery ("H2O2U," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. This policy is written to align with the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA).

Information We Collect

We collect the information you choose to give us, primarily through our quote request form: your name, phone number, email address, delivery location, the type of service you need, and any details you include in your message. If you call or email us, we keep the information you share in that exchange. Our site may also collect basic technical data such as your browser type and pages visited through standard analytics or server logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your request, provide quotes, schedule and complete water deliveries, communicate with you about your service, and keep records of our work. We do not sell your personal information.

How Your Information Is Shared

We share information only as needed to run our business. Form submissions are processed by Formspree, our form delivery provider, which transmits your message to us. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. These providers handle data on our behalf and under their own terms. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies

Our site uses minimal cookies, mainly for basic functionality and, where applicable, analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may affect some site features.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal, accounting, or recordkeeping requirements, after which we delete or de-identify it.

Your Rights

Texas residents may have the right to confirm whether we process their personal data, to access or correct it, to request deletion, and to appeal a decision. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information? Call us at (512) 695-5204 or reach out through our contact page.
